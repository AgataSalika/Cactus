CARE AND HEALTH FOR
YOUR CACTUS

  • In cold times add water once per a month and during summer do it when the soil is very dry.

  • Have good drenage so that the cactus does not accumulate water.

  • Place your cactus in a location with indirect light.

  • Does not water more than necessary. Overwatering can cause root rot.

  • Does not expose to high temperature.
